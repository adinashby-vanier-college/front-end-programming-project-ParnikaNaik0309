About Me

Hi, I'm Parnika Naik, an aspiring software developer currently pursuing a diploma in Software Development at Vanier College in Montreal, Canada. With a passion for technology and a keen eye for problem-solving, I'm dedicated to crafting innovative and user-friendly software solutions.

My journey into the world of programming began during my university days in India. Since then, I've been immersed in learning various programming languages and technologies, including Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and React.

Through my studies at Vanier College, I've gained hands-on experience in web development, mobile app development, data analysis, and machine learning. I'm eager to apply my knowledge and skills to real-world projects and contribute to the development of cutting-edge applications.

Beyond coding, I'm passionate about gaming, music, sports, and volunteering. I believe that a well-rounded individual is a better problem-solver, and I strive to maintain a balance between my academic and personal pursuits.

I'm excited to connect with like-minded individuals and explore new opportunities in the tech industry. Feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on social media.
